## What CookTwo is

CookTwo is a shared food system for two people. One kitchen, two different goals. You and your partner (or just you, on your own) share a single grocery list, a single pantry, and a single AI meal planner — and the app figures out how to make dinner work for *both* of you from one pan.

If you've just downloaded it, here's the short version of how it's organised:

- **Shopping tab** — a real-time shared grocery list that syncs between both of you in under a second.
- **Pantry tab** — a typed list of what's in your kitchen, so the AI can suggest meals using food you already have.
- **Meals tab** — a chat with the AI that generates dinner ideas, splits the recipe into two plates with different macros for each of you, and adds anything you're missing to the shopping list.
- **Profiles tab** — your diet, your goals, your body profile, and the plan you're on (Free or Premium).

Everything is partner-coloured. **Alex is sage green, Jordan is terracotta** — every item, every action, every notification has a small dot showing who added it.

That's the whole shape. Let's walk through every screen.

**What "to review" actually means.** The AI parses items in two passes. First it tries to categorise based on the name. Then it asks the AI model to re-check the ones it's not sure about. The "to review" badge means the AI flagged it for a second look. The `Ask AI to sort` button is that second look.
</GuideCallout>

## Common questions

**Can I use CookTwo solo?**
Yes. Tap "Cooking solo" on the welcome screen. You get a household of one. Everything works the same — you just don't have a second partner colour.

**What if my partner and I have very different diets (e.g. vegan + omnivore)?**
The AI handles it. Each of you sets your own diet in the Profiles tab. The adaptive plating adjusts portions to fit both. Ingredients that don't fit one partner's diet show up as "missing" for them but not the other.

**Why are my macros different from my partner's?**
Same recipe, different portions. The AI sizes each plate to match that person's goal (lose / maintain / gain) and TDEE. You cook the same pan.

**Can I undo a delete?**
Not yet. Be careful with the × button. We have it on the roadmap.

**Where is my data stored?**
On Cloudflare's D1 database, associated with your household. Encrypted in transit and at rest. You can sign out and delete your data by emailing us.

**Does it work on desktop?**
Yes — the PWA runs in any modern browser. Pin it to your dock or taskbar for an app-like experience.

**Is there a dark mode?**
Not yet. Cream and sage are the brand. Dark mode is on the roadmap for late 2026.

**What about Apple Watch / Wear OS?**
Not yet. The PWA is mobile-first for now.
